    • Parallax Narrative

    Create a narrative using the mechanic of css parallax scrolling using your custom boilerplate (html5 boiler plate, webpack, dart-sass) . Topic/story is up to you but create something you would like to share with world (i.e. globe worthy). Add google analytics to your page. Share with your friends, family, and social networks. We will see how far your story spread across the world at the end of the semester by creating a pdf usage report and sharing it with class.
